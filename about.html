    About the site and frequently asked questions

    Frequently asked questions

    What is the purpose of this site?

    As stated on the main page, this site hosts modified IPA files (that means iOS apps) with the purpose of bypassing Mobile Device Management (MDM for short) restrictions.

    Why would I want to use this site?

    In case a certain app you want to use (e. g. a game or social media) is restricted by MDM, you can obtain a modified version from here, which won't be picked up.

    What do I do with these files?

    They're IPAs. You can install them on your iDevice with an external sideloading service, such as AltStore or Scarlet

    Why are the app details different? Who is neoname and why are they part of every bundle ID?

    Restrictions are based on bundle IDs, app categories and Springboard names. These are the 3 values that are modified on these IPAs. I write my own username (neoname, that's me!) on each IPA because it's easier to differentiate from an unmodified one and to indicate that I was the one who modified it.
